Seaweed Salt

Natural 90g

Organic table salt made with 30% sugar kelp. A good replacement for ordinary salt, simultaneously providing the nutrients contained in the sugar kelp and extra umami taste. Can be used wherever you would normally use salt.

Ingredients

70 % Salt and 29,94 % sugar kelp (Saccharina latissima, organically farmed in Norway) og potetstivelse.
